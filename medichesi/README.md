# Medic HESI Study App

Single-file HTML study tool for the Paramedic HESI Capstone exam. Covers Airway, Cardiology, Trauma/Neurology, Medical/Special Populations, and EMS Operations. Flashcards for facts, scenarios for decision-making. Progress saves to the browser.

## How to host it (GitHub Pages)

1. Create a new GitHub repo (can be public or private, Pages works on both for free now)
2. Upload `index.html` to the root of the repo
3. Go to **Settings > Pages**
4. Under **Source**, pick **Deploy from a branch**
5. Pick **main** branch, folder **/ (root)**, hit **Save**
6. Wait a minute. Your URL will be: `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/REPO-NAME/`
7. Send AJ the link

Takes about 90 seconds once the repo is set up.

## What it does

- **Dashboard**: Overall progress plus per-section progress bars
- **Flashcards**: Tap to flip, mark as "got it" or "need to review"
- **Scenarios**: Multiple choice with detailed explanations after each answer
- **Persistence**: LocalStorage saves his place across sessions
- **Mobile-first**: Built to grind on a phone during downtime

## Content coverage

- Airway, Respiration & Ventilation (14 cards, 6 scenarios)
- Cardiology (12 cards, 6 scenarios)
- Trauma & Neurology (12 cards, 6 scenarios)
- Medical & Special Populations (15 cards, 6 scenarios)
- EMS Operations (12 cards, 6 scenarios)

Roughly 90 study items mapped directly to the HESI content outline.

## Disclaimer to pass along

This is a study aid, not an official NREMT or HESI question bank. Always follow local protocols and the official paramedic curriculum. Good luck.
